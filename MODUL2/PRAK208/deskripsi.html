Hearts2Hearts (하츠투하츠), also shortened as H2H, is an 8-member South Korean girl group under SM Entertainment. The members are: Jiwoo, Carmen, Yuha, Stella, Juun, A-na, Ian, and Ye-on. They debuted on February 24, 2025, with their first single album, The Chase.
Group Name Meaning: The name means they will connect with global fans through their own mysterious and beautiful music world filled with various emotions and heartfelt messages, and move forward together as a greater ‘us’. Official Greeting: “Hello, we are Hearts2Hearts!”

Hearts2Hearts Official Fandom Name: S2U (하츄)

Fandom Name Meaning: S2 has the shape of a heart, and since their group name has S2 in the middle, the “U” was added to symbolize the fans will always be at the center and will keep staying by their side.

Hearts2Hearts Official Color: Sky Blue

Hearts2Hearts Official Mascot: Falabella
